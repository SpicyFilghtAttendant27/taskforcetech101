Cybersecurity Tips

Stay safe online with these cybersecurity tips to protect yourself and your data.

Tip #1: Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Don't reuse passwords across multiple sites. Use a password manager to store and generate strong, unique passwords.

Tip #2: Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA adds an extra layer of protection to your online accounts by requiring two or more forms of verification.

Tip #3: Regularly Update Software

Keep your software, including antivirus programs, up to date to protect against vulnerabilities.
