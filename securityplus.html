CompTIA Security+ Certification

Your Gateway to a Cybersecurity Career

Overview

Security+ by CompTIA is a globally recognized certification that focuses on foundational cybersecurity skills. It is often the first step for individuals starting a career in IT security.

Key Domains

  1. Threats, Attacks, and Vulnerabilities
  2. Architecture and Design
  3. Implementation
  4. Operations and Incident Response
  5. Governance, Risk, and Compliance

Why Choose Security+?

  • Entry-level certification for individuals new to IT security.
  • Focuses on practical and hands-on skills.
  • Prepares you for job roles like security administrator, systems administrator, and network administrator.

Study Resources

  • CompTIA Official Security+ Page
  • CompTIA Security+ Certification Guide
  • Cybrary Security+ Training

Exam Details

Format Multiple-choice and performance-based questions
Questions Up to 90
Duration 90 minutes
Passing Score 750/900
Cost $392
