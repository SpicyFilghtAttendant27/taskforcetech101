Overview

The CompTIA Security+ certification is a globally recognized credential that validates the foundational skills necessary to perform core security functions and pursue an IT security career. It emphasizes hands-on practical skills, ensuring that security professionals are prepared to address a wide variety of issues.

Key Domains

The Security+ certification covers the following domains:

  • Attacks, Threats, and Vulnerabilities: Analyzing indicators of compromise and determining types of malware, attacks, and vulnerabilities.
  • Architecture and Design: Implementing secure network architecture concepts and systems design.
  • Implementation: Installing, configuring, and deploying network components while assessing and troubleshooting issues to support organizational security.
  • Operations and Incident Response: Monitoring and securing hybrid environments, including cloud, mobile, and IoT, and operating with an awareness of applicable regulations and policies.
  • Governance, Risk, and Compliance: Implementing and summarizing risk management best practices and understanding the business impact.

Why Choose CompTIA Security+?

  • Globally Recognized Credential: Validates your expertise and is respected by employers worldwide.
  • Vendor-Neutral Certification: Equips you with skills applicable across various platforms and technologies.
  • Compliance with Industry Standards: Approved by the U.S. Department of Defense to meet directive 8140/8570.01-M requirements.
  • Foundation for Advanced Certifications: Serves as a springboard for intermediate-level cybersecurity jobs and advanced certifications.

Exam Details

Exam Code SY0-701
Format Multiple-choice and performance-based questions
Number of Questions Maximum of 90
Duration 90 minutes
Passing Score 750 on a scale of 100-900
Recommended Experience At least two years of work experience in IT with a security focus
